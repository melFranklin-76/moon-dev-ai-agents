# 📋 Trading Rules — $250 Small Account

These are the non-negotiable rules for a $250 Webull cash account, Level 2 options.
The dashboard enforces several of these automatically.

---

## Account Setup

| Item | Value |
|------|-------|
| Broker | Webull |
| Account type | Cash account |
| Options level | Level 2 (buy calls/puts only) |
| Starting balance | $250 |
| Daily P&L goal | +$25 (+10%) |
| PDT rule | Exempt (cash account) |

---

## Trade Sizing

- **Max per trade:** 10–15% of account ($25–$37 at $250)
- **Max contracts:** 1 (at this account size, never more)
- **Never average down** — one entry, one exit
- **Premium range:** $0.10–$0.50 per share ($10–$50 total cost)

---

## Entry Rules — All Must Be True

- [ ] Market regime: SPY/QQQ above 20-day MA (or strong catalyst overrides)
- [ ] Catalyst Grader score: **A or A+ only**
- [ ] Signal score: **≥ 7/10** in Simple Mode (or green RS + IV CHEAP/FAIR in Pro)
- [ ] Earnings: **CLEAR or WATCH** — never CAUTION, HIGH RISK, or DANGER
- [ ] Time: **9:30–10:00 AM CT** (10:30–11:00 AM ET) — momentum window only
- [ ] Volume: stock showing 2x+ relative volume
- [ ] Price: stock $2–$100

---

## Exit Rules

| Trigger | Action |
|---------|--------|
| Premium up +15% | Sell half (lock in first win) |
| Premium up +25% | Sell rest (full exit) |
| Premium down -30% | Sell everything — no exceptions |
| 9:30 AM CT passes | Exit if not already up +15% |
| Setup breaks down | Exit immediately — don't wait for stop |

**The -30% stop is automatic discipline.** If you feel like waiting for it to come back, that's hope trading — exit now.

---

## Three-Strike Rule

Three red trades in one day = **done for the day**. The journal tab locks automatically.

- 1st red: warning, reduce size on next trade
- 2nd red: A+ setups only, smallest size possible
- 3rd red: close charts, close Webull, done

This rule exists because most account blowups happen on day 3+ of a losing streak, not day 1.

---

## Strategy Filters

The dashboard scans for three specific setups:

**#163 VWAP Reclaim (Long)**
- Price was below VWAP, crosses above with volume
- Volume burst = at least 1.2x the 10-bar average
- Buy ATM call immediately after the cross candle closes

**#172 Whole-Dollar Break**
- Price breaks and holds above a whole number ($10, $11, $12 etc.)
- Two consecutive closes above the level
- Buy ATM call after second confirmation candle

**#177 BTC Sync**
- Stock (MARA, MSTR, RIOT etc.) correlating to BTC moves
- BTC moving up + stock moving up = momentum confirmation
- Use BTC tab in Market Regime to check direction first

---

## What to Avoid

| Situation | Why |
|-----------|-----|
| Earnings within 7 days | IV crush kills premium overnight |
| IV RICH (ratio > 1.4x) | Overpaying — need massive move to profit |
| RS score < 5 | Stock lagging the market — weak setup |
| After 9:30 AM CT | Theta decay accelerates, momentum fades |
| Squeeze score < 25 | No short squeeze catalyst |
| Spread > 20% | Can't get filled at a fair price |
| 2nd or 3rd trade on a losing day | Revenge trading — biggest account killer |

## The Goal

**10-Day Prove-It Challenge:** 10 green days in a row.
- Green day = net positive P&L for the day
- One red day resets the streak to zero
- Track in Performance tab

Target account growth: $250 → $2,000 before applying for Level 3 / margin.
At $2,000, unlock XSP put credit spreads (income strategy, lower risk).

---

## Mindset Rules

1. **You are not right until the market agrees.** A good setup that goes red is still a loss — cut it.
2. **The best trade is sometimes no trade.** Sit on your hands if nothing meets all criteria.
3. **Log every trade.** Patterns you don't track will repeat and destroy your account.
4. **One red day is normal. Three in a row is a system problem.** Review your tendencies log.
5. **The dashboard scores setups. You make decisions.** Don't override a 3/10 score because you "feel good" about it.
