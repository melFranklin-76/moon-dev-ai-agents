Your 20 best-of-the-best strategies. Use as a pre-trade checklist.

## 🚀 Momentum + Confirmation (159–162)

### #159 — 1-Minute ORB + VWAP Hold
**Setup:** Stock breaks the opening range high (first 1-min candle) AND holds above VWAP.
**Entry:** Buy ATM call after 2nd 1-min candle confirms hold above range high.
**Stop:** Close back below opening range high OR -30% premium.
**Target:** +15% first, +25% full exit.
**Best time:** 9:30–9:45 AM CT only.

### #160 — 1-Minute ORB Fail → VWAP Reject
**Setup:** Stock breaks opening range high then immediately fails and drops below VWAP.
**Entry:** Buy ATM put after price confirms below VWAP (not just a wick).
**Stop:** Reclaim above opening range high OR -30% premium.
**Target:** +15% first, +25% full exit.

### #161 — 5-Minute OR Break + 1-Minute Flag
**Setup:** Stock breaks 5-min opening range, pulls back to form a 1-min bull flag, then breaks flag high.
**Entry:** Buy call on flag breakout candle close.
**Stop:** Below flag low OR -30% premium.
**Target:** Measure flag pole height — project same distance up. Or +25%.

### #162 — Premarket High Retest-and-Go
**Setup:** Stock gaps up, pulls back to test premarket high, holds, and continues up.
**Entry:** Buy ATM call when price bounces off premarket high with volume.
**Stop:** Below premarket high OR -30% premium.
**Target:** +15% first, +25% full exit.

## 💧 VWAP Edge (163–165)

### #163 — VWAP Reclaim (Long) ✅ SCANNER ACTIVE
**Setup:** Price was below VWAP, crosses above with a volume burst (≥1.2x recent average).
**Entry:** Buy ATM call immediately after reclaim candle closes above VWAP.
**Stop:** Close back below VWAP OR -30% premium.
**Target:** +15% first, +25% full exit.
**Note:** Dashboard auto-detects this. Add ticker to watchlist to get live alerts.

### #164 — VWAP Rejection (Short)
**Setup:** Price approaches VWAP from below, wicks above it, immediately fails back down.
**Entry:** Buy ATM put after failed VWAP test candle closes.
**Stop:** Sustained close above VWAP OR -30% premium.
**Target:** +15% first, +25% full exit.

### #165 — VWAP Micro Double Bottom/Top
**Setup:** Price touches VWAP, bounces, pulls back to VWAP a second time, holds again.
**Entry (long):** Buy call on second VWAP bounce with volume confirmation.
**Entry (short):** Buy put on second VWAP rejection.
**Stop:** Break through VWAP on the third touch OR -30% premium.
**Target:** +25%.

## 📈 Pullback Continuations (166–168)

### #166 — 9/20 EMA Pullback Long
**Setup:** Uptrending stock (9 EMA above 20 EMA) pulls back to touch the 9 or 20 EMA, then shows a reversal candle.
**Entry:** Buy call on the reversal candle close.
**Stop:** Close below the 20 EMA OR -30% premium.
**Target:** +25%.

### #167 — 9/20 EMA Pullback Short
**Setup:** Downtrending stock (9 EMA below 20 EMA) rallies up to test the 9 or 20 EMA, then fails.
**Entry:** Buy put after rejection candle close.
**Stop:** Close above the 20 EMA OR -30% premium.
**Target:** +25%.

### #168 — Higher-Low / Lower-High
**Setup (long):** Stock making higher lows — each pullback holds above the previous low.
**Setup (short):** Stock making lower highs — each bounce fails below the previous high.
**Entry:** Buy call/put at the third touch of the trend.
**Stop:** Break of the trend structure OR -30% premium.
**Target:** +25%.

## 🔄 Reversal / Mean-Reversion (169–171)

### #169 — Stop-Run Reclaim
**Setup:** Price briefly dips below a key level (taking out stops), then immediately reclaims above it with strong volume.
**Entry:** Buy call on the reclaim candle close.
**Stop:** Second close below the key level OR -30% premium.
**Target:** +15% first, +25% full exit.

### #170 — Exhaustion Wick Reversal
**Setup:** Long wick candle (wick = 2x+ body) at an extreme level. Price wicks hard in one direction but closes near the open.
**Entry:** Buy put (if upper wick) or call (if lower wick) after the exhaustion candle closes.
**Stop:** New extreme beyond the wick OR -30% premium.
**Target:** +25%.

### #171 — Bollinger Squeeze Release
**Setup:** Bollinger Bands squeeze tight (low volatility). Price breaks outside the band with a strong candle.
**Entry:** Buy call (upper break) or put (lower break) on the breakout candle close.
**Stop:** Candle closes back inside bands OR -30% premium.
**Target:** +25–50% — squeeze releases can be explosive.

## 💰 Price Levels & Auction Theory (172–175)

### #172 — Whole-Dollar Break-and-Hold ✅ SCANNER ACTIVE
**Setup:** Stock breaks and holds above a whole-number price level ($10, $11, etc.) for at least 2 consecutive 1-min candles.
**Entry:** Buy ATM call after second confirmation close above the whole number.
**Stop:** Close back below the whole number OR -30% premium.
**Target:** +15% first, +25% full exit.
**Note:** Dashboard auto-detects this.

### #173 — Half-Dollar Rejection Fade
**Setup:** Stock rallies into a half-dollar level ($10.50, $11.50 etc.) and stalls or reverses.
**Entry:** Buy put after a clear rejection candle closes below the half-dollar.
**Stop:** Close above the half-dollar OR -30% premium.
**Target:** +25%.

### #174 — POC Reclaim/Loss
**Setup:** Price reclaims or loses the Point of Control (POC — highest volume price level of the day).
**Entry:** Buy call on POC reclaim with volume. Buy put on POC loss.
**Stop:** Reverse through POC again OR -30% premium.
**Target:** +25%.

### #175 — LVN Slide
**Setup:** Price enters a Low Volume Node (LVN — thin area on the volume profile) and accelerates through it.
**Entry:** Buy in the direction of acceleration as price enters the LVN.
**Stop:** Price stalls or reverses back out of LVN OR -30% premium.
**Target:** Next HVN (high volume node) or +30%.

## 🔗 Correlation (176–177)

### #176 — Index-Lead Sync (SPY/QQQ)
**Setup:** SPY or QQQ makes a strong directional move. A correlated stock has not yet moved.
**Entry:** Buy call/put on the lagging stock in the same direction as the index.
**Stop:** Index reverses OR -30% premium.
**Target:** +25% or when stock catches up to index move.

### #177 — BTC-Lead Sync (MARA/RIOT/MSTR) ✅ SCANNER ACTIVE
**Setup:** BTC makes a strong 1-min move. BTC-correlated stocks (MARA, RIOT, MSTR, CLSK) lag.
**Entry:** Buy call (BTC up) or put (BTC down) on the lagging stock.
**Stop:** BTC reverses OR -30% premium.
**Target:** +25%.
**Note:** Check BTC price in Market Regime tab before entering.

## ⏰ Time-of-Day (178)

### #178 — Golden-Hour Range Break
**Setup:** Market has been consolidating for 30–60 min (roughly 10:00–10:30 AM CT). Stock breaks the consolidation range with volume.
**Entry:** Buy call (upside break) or put (downside break) after breakout candle closes outside the range.
**Stop:** Candle closes back inside range OR -30% premium.
**Target:** +25–30%. Range breaks after a consolidation period can trend well.
